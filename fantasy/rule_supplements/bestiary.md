## Abreviations and formatting
- H = hearts
- AV = armor value, flat reduction of damage
- DMG = Damage
- X2 = multi-attack with 2 attacks

## Tags

### Type
- **Magic** - Can cast spells or use magic abilities
- **Feral** - Non-sentient creature
- **Corrupt** - Organic magic
- **Construct** - Inorganic entity
- **Spirit** - Natural and humanoid spirits
- **Demon** - evil entities
- **Celestial** - avatars and minions of celestial entities

### Power archetypes
- **Pest** - Nuisance unless in large numbers
- **Mook** - Weak foot soldier 
- **Elite** - Powerful foot soldier
- **Boss** - Strongest

### Tactical archetypes
- **Striker** - High mobility with critical
- **Skirmisher** - Mix of ranged and melee
- **Brawler** - Mix of DPS and defense
- **Support** - Indirect help through buff/healing/debuff
- **Tank** - Max defense
- **Glass Canon** - All firepower
